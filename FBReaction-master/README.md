# FBReaction

## Facebook-like "Like with Reaction" demo for Android


![](https://github.com/dbof10/FBReaction/blob/master/assets/demo.gif)  

Just like Facebook's app:
- Press on like button
- Keep your finger down, swipe left or right
- Release your finger
